Our Community Earthquakers Robotics values our community, and we reach out as much as possible to stay involved and present within our school, neighborhood, and wider Philadelphia area.

At Friends' Central, community is highly valued, and we reflect that in our robotics team. Whether it's reaching out to middle school students, presenting our robot to the high school body, or collaborating with another school, Earthquakers Robotics is always looking for an opportunity to reach out and engage.
2023 Outreach In 2023, we have been active in our community and made an effort to involve technology and education within our outreach work.
