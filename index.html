Our Mission
     The Earthquakers, established in the fall of 2022, are the         robotics team representing Friends' Central School. Friends' Central School is a Quaker, community-based school. The Quaker values of simplicity, peace, equality, and integrity guide our interactions with ourselves and our community. In founding a FIRST Robotics team, we incorporate these ideas into FIRST’s mission of inspiring students to pursue careers in science and technology. We hope that by creating an accessible, collaborative robotics program, we can develop students with the skill sets to affect change in the world around them.
          The FCS robotics team was created to inspire future, present,         
and past FCS students to think creatively and freely.
