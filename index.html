Our Mission
     The Earthquakers, established in the fall of 2022, are the robotics team representing Friends Central School. FCS is based in Lower Merion, Philadelphia, and has been educating since 1845. As a community-based school, we strive to inspire and give back, and through our robotics team, we hope to illuminate technology as part of our school's curriculum. Our team's mission is to bring robotics and FIRST technology to Friends Central and our surrounding community.           The FCS robotics team was created to inspire future, present,         
and past FCS students to think creatively and freely.
