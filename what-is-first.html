What is FIRST Robotics? FIRST Robotics is an international robotics competition that challenges high school students to design, build, and program their own robot. FIRST stands for "For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology."

FIRST Robotics mission is to inspire young people to be science and technology leaders and innovators by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, inspire innovation, and foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.
FRC
We participate in FRC, which stands for the First Robotics Competition. FRC is the high school-level competition within FIRST and takes place at multiple schools across both coasts.

The championship event takes place in April and culminates the work done by students over the course of the season.

Students are given the resources they need to create their robot and are inspired to create a unique solution for each year's competition.
